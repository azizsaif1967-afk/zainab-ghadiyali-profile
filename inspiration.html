Inspiration in Practice

Quotes are only useful when you put them to work. Three small rituals borrowed from Zainab's own habits.

01

One quote, one week

Pick a single quote on Monday. Write it on a sticky note. Let it shape how you answer the week's hardest question.

02

Three books at a time

Borrowed from Zainab herself — one technical, one historical, one ridiculous. The cross-pollination is where the ideas live.

03

Share, don't hoard

Pass a quote to a friend you've been meaning to text. A small act of generosity is the cheapest leadership move there is.
